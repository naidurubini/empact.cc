A social media for social good integrating empathy, action, and impact to solve the most pressing problems.

story-based empathy

Share your story. View stories of others. We humanize existing movements and surface emerging movements through narratives directly from the source.

grassroots action

We make activism more accessible by allowing you to select from trusted actions online or locally. We also think communities are most informed about their needs—and enable individuals to share their own calls to action.

trackable impact

Our platform not only aggregates social movements, but we also track how bottom-up efforts lead to tangible impact. Research and cross-sector collaboration are our friends.

We’re just getting started. Our focus is first on the below U.S. based movements. We will certainly be expanding.
